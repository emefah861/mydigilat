About the service

DigiLAT is a free service that provides plain-English legal information to people in the United Kingdom. When you tell us about your situation, we send you an overview of the UK laws that may apply, the rights you may have, and template letters you can use — all at no cost.

Yes, completely free. There are no hidden charges, no premium tiers, and no subscription. We will never ask you for payment.

What we provide

No. DigiLAT provides general legal information, not legal advice. We explain how UK law may relate to your situation in general terms. Our service does not create a solicitor-client relationship, and the information we provide should not be treated as a substitute for professional legal advice.

You will receive a clear overview that typically includes: the areas of UK law that may be relevant to your situation, an explanation of the rights that are generally associated with those areas, and outline of how similar situations are commonly understood in a legal context, and where appropriate template letters or email that can be adapted for reference.

We cover a range of common legal issues including consumer rights, tenant and housing disputes, employment law, debt and finance, discrimination, and contract disputes. If your issue falls outside these areas, we will do our best to point you towards the right resource.

If your situation is urgent or involves court proceedings, we recommend seeking professional legal advice. We can refer you to qualified solicitors in our pro bono network, or signpost you to free services such as Citizens Advice, Law Centres, or the government's legal aid scheme.
